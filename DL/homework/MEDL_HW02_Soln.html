# Solution
x = tf.Variable([[0], [0], [0]], dtype=tf.float32)
A = tf.reshape(A_unshaped, [3, 3])
b = tf.reshape(b_unshaped, [3, 1])
LearningRate = 0.1

cost = tf.reduce_mean(tf.math.square(tf.matmul(A, x) - b))
optm = tf.train.GradientDescentOptimizer(learning_rate=LearningRate).minimize(cost)
init = tf.global_variables_initializer()

sess = tf.Session()
sess.run(init)

for _ in range(500):
    sess.run(optm)

Here is the shape of $\omega$ and $x$.


$$ \begin{align*} \omega &= \begin{bmatrix} \omega_0 \\ \omega_1 \\ \omega_2\end{bmatrix}, \qquad x = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ x_1 \\ x_2\end{bmatrix}\\ \\ X &= \begin{bmatrix} \left(x^{(1)}\right)^T \\ \left(x^{(2)}\right)^T \\ \left(x^{(3)}\right)^T \\ \vdots\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & x_1^{(1)} & x_2^{(1)} \\ 1 & x_1^{(2)} & x_2^{(2)} \\ 1 & x_1^{(3)} & x_2^{(3)} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\\end{bmatrix}, \qquad y = \begin{bmatrix} y^{(1)}\\ y^{(2)} \\y^{(3)} \\ \vdots \end{bmatrix} \end{align*} $$

(1) [hand written] Write the gradient of log likelihood.

Solution $$\nabla \ell(\omega) = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{\partial \ell(\omega)}{\partial \omega_0} \\ \frac{\partial \ell(\omega)}{\partial \omega_1} \\ \frac{\partial \ell(\omega)}{\partial \omega_2} \end{bmatrix} = X^T \left(y-h_{\omega}(x)\right) = X^T \left(y-\sigma(X \omega)\right) $$

def h(x,w):
